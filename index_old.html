Our Vision

BrainGnosis Inc. is dedicated to creating smarter, more reliable, and human-centric AI solutions for organizations. Our mission is to create smarter AI for humans.

Specifically, we offer products featuring bespoke AI Agents that enhance productivity and automate scientific, regulatory, and business processes. We integrate your organizational processes and tools with a robust and scalable AI agentic framework to develop and automate workflows.

Our Software Development Kit is akin to an operating system for AI agents, where we train and develop agents specific to your needs using multimodal large language models for planning and executing tasks within your organization.

We have incorporated memory, state, and context-specific domains to provide our customers with robust and verifiable benchmark solutions. Our goal is to make smarter, scalable, robust, and consistent AI agents the backbone of your organization.
