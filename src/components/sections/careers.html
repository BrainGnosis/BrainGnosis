About the Role

We're looking for a mission-driven UI Developer to join our startup team and help build the next generation of AI agent interfaces. You'll work closely with our product and engineering teams to create intuitive, powerful user experiences for AgentOS. In our startup environment, you'll have the opportunity to wear multiple hats and make a significant impact across different areas of the product.

What You'll Do
  • Design and implement responsive, accessible user interfaces
  • Collaborate with designers to translate mockups into pixel-perfect implementations
  • Build reusable component libraries and design systems
  • Optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability
  • Work with AI/ML teams to create intuitive interfaces for complex agent workflows
  • Contribute to product strategy and user experience decisions in a startup environment
  • Wear multiple hats - from frontend development to UX research and product feedback
What We're Looking For
  • 1-2 years of experience with modern JavaScript frameworks (React, Vue, or Angular)
  • Strong proficiency in HTML5, CSS3, and responsive design principles
  • Experience with design systems and component libraries
  • Knowledge of accessibility standards and best practices
  • Passion for creating exceptional user experiences
  • Mission-driven mindset and willingness to contribute beyond just coding
  • Startup mentality - adaptable, resourceful, and excited about wearing multiple hats
Bonus Points
  • • Experience with AI/ML product interfaces
  • • Knowledge of data visualization libraries (D3.js, Chart.js)
  • • Familiarity with Python and backend technologies
  • • Experience in healthcare or scientific software

About the Role

We're looking for a passionate Cloud Developer to join our fast-growing AI startup. You'll be responsible for deploying AI features to the cloud and building scalable solutions. This role is perfect for someone eager to learn and grow in cloud technologies while wearing multiple hats in a startup environment.

What You'll Do
  • Deploy and maintain AI models and features on major cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • Build and optimize cloud infrastructure for scalable services
  • Implement CI/CD pipelines for rapid deployment
  • Design and maintain APIs for cloud-based services
  • Monitor system performance and scaling
  • Ensure security best practices for cloud deployments
  • Collaborate with AI/ML team to deploy models
  • Support DevOps practices and infrastructure as code
What We're Looking For
  • 1-2 years of experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP) or strong eagerness to learn
  • Basic knowledge of containerization (Docker, Kubernetes) with willingness to grow
  • Programming skills in Python, Node.js, or Go or desire to learn
  • Understanding of API development and microservices or curiosity to explore
  • Passion for cloud technologies and infrastructure
  • Mission-driven mindset and eagerness to learn new technologies
  • Strong problem-solving skills and growth mindset
Bonus Points
  • Experience with serverless computing (Lambda, Cloud Functions)
  • Knowledge of ML model deployment and MLOps practices
  • Previous startup or fast-paced environment experience
  • Understanding of cloud security and compliance
