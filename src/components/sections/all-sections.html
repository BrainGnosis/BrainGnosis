Why Linkton

LLMs Alone Are Not Enough

LLMs can generate plans, but they do not reliably execute in the physical world without structure, constraints, and grounded state. In scientific automation, “good enough” is not good enough.

The Systems Layer Is the Moat

Foundation models provide general reasoning. Our focus is the hard problem: instrument abstraction, bidirectional control, sensor ingestion, orchestration, validation, and deployment into real environments.

Extensible Across Configurations

Not one robot driving one setup. An extensible platform deployed across many different instrument configurations and organizational constraints — from pharma R&D to healthcare operations and regulatory compliance.

Transparent & Controllable

Systems must be deterministic where needed, verifiable, and safe. Full audit trails, compliance-grade traceability, and granular control over every agent decision.
