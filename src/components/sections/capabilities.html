How LINK Works

A governed workflow engine for real-world AI execution

LINK is built as an operational graph: it ingests context, plans safely, executes tasks, validates outcomes, and escalates to humans when confidence drops.

01 Ingest

Unify Context

Pull structured and unstructured context from lab systems, SOP repositories, and operational databases.

02 Plan

Constrain Agent Decisions

Translate goals into bounded execution plans with policy checks, risk thresholds, and deterministic gates.

03 Execute

Orchestrate Tools and Instruments

Dispatch tasks across APIs, enterprise tools, and physical systems while preserving state between steps.

04 Observe

Track Outcomes in Real Time

Continuously monitor signals, compare against expected ranges, and capture context for downstream review.

05 Validate

Enforce Quality Controls

Apply domain rules, benchmark policies, and procedural constraints before outcomes are accepted.

06 Escalate

Route to Human Oversight

Trigger reviewer interventions with full context when confidence, policy, or safety conditions require manual control.
