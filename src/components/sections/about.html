About Linkton

Making researchers dramatically more productive

Across pharma, healthcare, and regulatory, the bottleneck is not generating text or code. It is reliably executing, observing, and iterating workflows across complex, heterogeneous environments.

Linkton is building the systems layer that bridges AI intelligence with the physical world — turning experimental, clinical, and regulatory workflows into repeatable, auditable closed-loop runs. We are starting with pharma R&D and extending into healthcare and regulatory operations.

Works across arbitrary instrument setups — not tied to one hardware stack
