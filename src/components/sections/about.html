Who We Are

BrainGnosis Inc. is on a mission to build reliable, adaptable, and deeply human-aligned AI agents that transform how organizations work. Our name combines "brain" with the Greek word gnōsis (γνῶσις), meaning "knowledge" or "insight."

This reflects our vision of AI that goes beyond data processing. We build AI that gains gnostic-level understanding, operating with intuition, memory, and context, just like the human brain.

Introducing AgentOS

AgentOS is our vertical-agnostic neurosymbolic AI platform. It combines neural and symbolic intelligence to create structured, intelligent agents that can collaborate, remember context, and execute complex business workflows while remaining transparent and controllable.
