The Role

Deploy AI models and build scalable cloud infrastructure for our cutting-edge AI platform. Work directly with founders in a startup environment where you'll wear multiple hats and make real impact.

You'll Do

  • • Deploy AI models on AWS/Azure/GCP
  • • Build CI/CD pipelines & infrastructure
  • • Design APIs & microservices
  • • Monitor performance & scaling
  • • Ensure security best practices
  • • Collaborate with AI/ML team on model deployment

You Have

  • • 1-2 years cloud platform experience
  • • Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform/IaC
  • • Python, Node.js, or Go programming
  • • CI/CD tools (GitHub Actions, Jenkins)
  • • API development & microservices
  • • Basic database knowledge (SQL/NoSQL)
  • • Monitoring & logging tools familiarity
  • • Mission-driven startup mindset

Bonus: Serverless computing, MLOps practices, load balancing, cloud security, or AI/ML workflow experience

Why This Role Matters

You'll be building the infrastructure that powers AI systems helping researchers cure diseases, scientists make discoveries, and enterprises work smarter. Your cloud architecture directly enables breakthrough AI applications.

AI/ML Infrastructure

Master deploying and scaling AI models in production cloud environments.

Startup Growth

Build cloud systems from the ground up in a fast-growing AI startup.

Direct Impact

Work directly with founders and architect the infrastructure that scales our AI platform.

Technical Leadership

Lead cloud architecture decisions and build your own DevOps team as we scale.
